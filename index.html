I'm a computer scientist at Data Science and Learning (DSL) division at Argonne National Laboratory. I also hold joint appointments in X-ray Science division (XSD) at Advanced Photon Source (APS) and Consortium for Advanced Science and Engineering (CASE) at the University of Chicago.
Currently, I'm working on large-scale high-performance systems that deal with compute- and data-intensive synchrotron radiation imaging problems.

  • High-End Computing Systems
